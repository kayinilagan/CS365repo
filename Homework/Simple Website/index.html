The Wrecks

The Wrecks have always been one of my favorite bands since discovering them in my junior year of high school. In particular, I love their songs: Favorite Liar, Revolution, and Feels So Nice the most. I've actually gotten tickets to see them coming this November so I am really excited to meet one of my all-time favorite bands!

Paramore

Growing up, Paramore has always been the band that was stuck in my head. I love how all their songs are such high energy, emotional, as well as just plain fun to listen to. Some of my favorites are: Still Into You, Ain't it Fun, and Brick by Boring Brick.

The Band CAMINO

I didn't realize how much I listened to The Band CAMINO until Spotify released their stats of each account publicly. Turns out, they are my #1 band of all-time on Spotify and for good reason too. I love their Indie/Alt-Rock vibe and all they songs can range from emotional solos to songs you'd play at a party. I love these songs by them: See Through, Roses, and I Think I Like You.
